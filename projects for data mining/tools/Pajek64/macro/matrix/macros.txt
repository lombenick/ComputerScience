When reading Ore graph
with Options/ReadWrite/ Ore: 1-Male, 2-Female links
the following relations are generated:

 1 - father of
 2 - mother of
 3 - spouse of

The rest can be obtained by running the macros:

 4 - parent of
 5 - child of 
 6 - son of
 7 - daughter of
 8 - husband of
 9 - wife of
10 - sibling of
11 - brother of
12 - sister of
13 - uncle of
14 - aunt of
15 - semisibling of
